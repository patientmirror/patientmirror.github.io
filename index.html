Patient MIRROR

Medical Instructional Resource Review Of Representation

About

Patient MIRROR is a research study examining demographic representation within clinical vignettes used in medical education and training. Clinical vignettes — the brief patient scenarios central to case-based learning — shape how future clinicians conceptualize disease presentation, risk, and treatment across different populations. This project systematically analyzes the demographic composition of such vignettes to identify patterns of representation and omission.
